About

More Than a Student Organization.

🎯

Our Mission

Empower aspiring programmers by providing opportunities for collaboration, innovation, and real-world experience that goes beyond the classroom.

🔭

Our Vision

To become the leading campus community that shapes the next generation of software engineers and technology leaders in the region.

📚

Learn

Gain practical knowledge through hands-on workshops, peer mentorship, and structured technical sessions at every skill level.

⚙️

Build

Work with teams to create meaningful applications and real-world projects that ship — not just sit in a repo.

Pillars

Four Foundations.

Learn

Workshops, mentorships, and technical sessions designed to sharpen your skills at every level — from beginner to advanced.

Build

Real-world projects that turn ideas into working software — shipped, not shelved. Every line matters.

Lead

Develop confidence, communication, and leadership through real teamwork, ownership, and accountability.

Connect

Build lasting friendships and professional networks that extend well beyond university and into your career.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

  • Join during the official recruitment period and complete the registration process. Watch our social media pages for announcements and opening dates each semester.
  • No experience required. We welcome complete beginners — our workshops are specifically designed to teach you from the ground up in a supportive, judgment-free environment.
  • Fees vary per academic year. Details are announced during the official recruitment period. Contact us through the form below for the current rate.
  • ASP is open to all year levels and all courses. We believe great ideas and great developers come from every corner of campus.
  • We run Coding Nights weekly, Tech Talks monthly, and larger events like hackathons and workshops each semester. There is always something happening.
